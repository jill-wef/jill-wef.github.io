October 2003

Guatemala

I made these pictures while I was on a Vision Quest Photo Workshop in Guatemala in 2003. These two young girls were celebrating "Day of the Dead" with their family in a cemetary of Guatemala. Their colorful traditional clothing, which is called "traje", may vary depending on the different villages you visit.

I met these three young ladies in the small impoverished town of Santa Maria de Jesus, Guatemala. As with many families there, the older girls serve as the "day care" while their mothers sell goods at market and their fathers work in the fields. This trip and these children added so much perspective to my life I will forever be grateful for that experience.
